Robotics

Below I present this project that I carry out under the Arduino technology, it is a quadruped robot with the characteristic that detects objects on the road and performs movements to avoid them, among its main characteristics is that of walking quietly, running, turning the right, turn left in addition to making movements that give more realism.

First stable version

In this video you can see a test carried out on the first stable version.

Resources for its construction

Materials
  • 8 ServoMotor SG90
  • 1 Mini ProtoBoard
  • 1 Ultrasonic HC_04
  • 1 power supply
  • cables 10cm and 20cm
CorelDraw structure for CNC

As a first resource I publish the design that I made in Corel Draw for the robot skeleton, in a new version that I have designed.

Scheme of Connection in Tinkercad

As a second resource, I share the wiring diagram of electronic components, just as required for the code to work properly.

Last comments

As advice for the assembly of this project I recommend using a PC power source, in addition to feeding it at 5 volts with a flow of 3000 amps, feeding the arduino at 5 volts independently in addition to gnd joints..
